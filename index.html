Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Spool Scanner does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Overview

Spool Scanner ("the App") is a filament spool NFC tag reader for 3D printing enthusiasts. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data to external servers

Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Scan history: Material type, color, weight, diameter, temperatures, and optional notes for each scanned filament spool.
  • App settings: Theme preference, clipboard/export field selections, scan behavior preferences.
  • Backup file: An automatic backup copy of your scan history, stored in the app's private data directory.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it (JSON or CSV) or share it using Android's share functionality.

NFC Usage

The App uses your device's NFC (Near Field Communication) hardware to read and, optionally, to write filament spool tags. The App:

  • Only accesses NFC when you initiate a scan or a write — never in the background.
  • Reads two kinds of tags:
    • Bambu Lab filament spool RFID tags (encrypted MIFARE Classic format).
    • NDEF tags (e.g. NTAG213 / NTAG215 / NTAG216) previously written by this app or another compatible tool.
  • Writes, only when you explicitly tap the “Write tag” button, to blank or rewritable NDEF tags. The data written consists solely of the filament details you have entered in the app (brand, material, color, weight, diameter, temperatures, optional notes, and an app-generated per-spool identifier). Bambu Lab tags are never written to or modified.
  • Does not transmit any NFC tag data to any server — all NFC interaction is purely between your phone and the tag in front of it.

Android Backup

If you have Google backup enabled on your device, the App's scan history may be included in your device backup per Android's standard backup mechanism. This is controlled by your device settings, not by the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are no network calls made by the App.

Data Sharing

The App does not share your data with any third parties. You can manually export your scan history as JSON or CSV files using the in-app export function. Any sharing of exported files is initiated and controlled entirely by you.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
